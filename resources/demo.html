[ top ] Material color palette

Colors take up the most space. The library with color will take 7.6K gzipped
And without color it will take 1.6K gzipped

Available color names are:
black white red pink purple deeppurple indigo blue lightblue cyan teal green lightgreen lime yellow amber orange deeporange brown grey bluegrey
Attach -back for it to work for the background
Attach -angry to override default styles of a given framework
Attach -t-1, -t-2,-t-3,-t-4,-t-4, -t-5 for tints
Attach -s-1, -s-2,-s-3,-s-4,-s-4, -s-5 for shades
Attach -a-1, -a-2,-a-3 for accents
